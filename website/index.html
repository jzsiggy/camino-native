Which customers haven't ordered in 90 days?
I'll find customers with no recent orders: SELECT name, email, MAX(order_date) AS last_order FROM customers JOIN orders USING(customer_id) GROUP BY name, email HAVING MAX(order_date) < NOW() - INTERVAL '90 days'; 23 inactive customers
